Welcome to the RGU Blacksmithing Society!

Whether you’re a seasoned blacksmith or just curious about the craft, you've found the right place to ignite your passion for metalwork. Here, we forge not just metal, but also friendships and skills that last a lifetime. Join us to learn traditional techniques, experiment with innovative ideas, and create impressive projects, all while enjoying the camaraderie of fellow enthusiasts. Our society is a hub for sharing knowledge, practicing the art, and having a great time. So, grab your hammer, light up the forge, and let’s make some sparks fly together! Join the RGU Blacksmithing Society today and transform your creativity into craftsmanship..
